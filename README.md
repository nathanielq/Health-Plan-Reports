# Health-Plan-Reports
Students at a school have a free period they self select into. This is saved into a report daily. Due to the natue of the free-period, teachers do not have health plan data for the students since they are not rostered in their classes. They would then not be aware of any allergies, or other health concerns of students they are responsible for. To provide a fix for this, we created a system that reads their health plans into a dataframe and combines the dataframe from the student free-period selection into one dataframe keying off of their student IDs. We then split by each teacher's name and send that teacher the report of health plans for the students they are responsible for in that period.
